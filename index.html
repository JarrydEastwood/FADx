FADX is the Faculty of Arts and Design’s annual graduate exhibition. It celebrates the achievements of our graduating students and allows them to display their best work for family, friends, industry, and the public.

Through FADX we encourage our students to reflect on their education and what they’ve achieved throughout their degree, who they are as emerging professionals and how to effectively communicate their accumulated knowledge and skills to prospective employers and industry contacts.
