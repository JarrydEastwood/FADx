Australian National University - Enquiry form

Designed for future enquiries, aimed to be more target selected within ANU to get prospective students to the correct person they need to talk to using Drupal Webform.

Personal information

Gathering general information on who the person inquiring ANU is.

Which of the below best describe your current situation?

Logic to understand the users' situation which opens up a new set of questions - changing depending on the box selected.

What best describes you?

Based off the previous selection different parts of the form opens up. Within this section there are 4 options which can be displayed, all of which are based of the selection in the previous section.

Designed and built by the 2023 Interaction Design graduates
